The world needs you!

And in order to address the challenges of our generation, it needs you to be enthusiastic, empowered, and exceptional.

We know that you want to live a vibrant life and make an impact. So, to support you in realizing your highest potential, your organization is collaborating with eleven others to host a three-day leadership retreat designed to:

  • Give you specific tools and practices for unleashing community and impact.
  • Create lasting friendships with conscious, community-minded students.
  • Inspire you, connect you, and sustain you in your personal leadership journey.

Meet and share with other college-aged leaders from across the country to level up your leadership, strengthen your interpersonal and team-building skills, and help catalyze your vibrant and supportive changemaker community.
