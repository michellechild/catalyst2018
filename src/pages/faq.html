    • {{else ifequal faq.question 'What will the accomodations be like?'}}
  • {{faq.question}}

    Everyone attending Catalyst 2018 will be staying in suites that each sleep 5 people. A suite consists of 2 double rooms and a single room with a shared kitchen and living space. You will find out who you are staying with when you arrive. We do our best to place people from the same organizations together- however, you may also be given the opportunity to stay with members of other organizations. If you have any concerns about lodging please do not hesitate to contact your staff contact or email support@catalyst2018.org.. We want to make sure that you feel safe and are comfortable with lodging. We will do our very best to address any special needs or concerns.
