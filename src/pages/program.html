The Context

We live in a moment of transition. Our institutions don’t deliver the benefits they once did (or seemed to) & it’s impossible to pretend they’re sustainable or inclusive. Young people everywhere are awakening to their larger missions & to the interconnectedness of life.

As we awaken to this interconnectedness, we see that we cannot change one system without changing the others that support it, and that all of our institutions reflect the dominant culture’s perceptions of the world. And so we are faced with challenging questions that have long, nuanced, & hotly debated answers:

How do we change?

How do we stop participating in civilization’s exploitative, exclusive, & degenerative systems?

We believe there’s a relatively simple answer to these questions: We BE the change we want to see. We choose to embody our values & visions, as individuals, as organizations, & as a community.

Catalyst 2018: Join us

So, we invite you to attend Catalyst 2018, a 4-day leadership retreat, as part of your journey; your journey to embody who you really are, who you wish to be, & who you are becoming. You have already met others of your tribe, recognized by common purpose, values, institutions, & by the similarity of the paths you have walked. As your paths cross more & more, the real change starts to happen & your passions combine & amplify.

We can help you meet others, learn from each other, & work together. We are a network of people & organizations dedicated to an inclusive, regenerative, & sustainable world. So while we may each have distinct goals & focuses in the world, we are all connected at the highest level.

We are working toward the same goal, so why do it separately?

The Goals

To help you launch a new stage of your leadership journey; to give clarity to your quest to co-create a more beautiful world. At this gathering, you will be equipped with next-level leadership skills that will enable you to:

  • Build community & maximize impact.
  • Be the leader your community, organization, & the world needs.

The Skills

Mobilizing, Organizing, Marketing, & Fundraising for a Cause

Learn how to:

  • Form, inspire, & retain excellent teams in order to reach big goals.
  • Delegate, set milestones. & celebrate successes to reach goals without “burnout.”
  • Create a process for succession planning that ‍‍‍‍sets up future leaders for success.
  • Grow your impact through partnerships and/or coalitions.

Identity, Purpose, Life Balance, & Personal Development

Develop an awareness of:

  • Your personal story & how it shapes how you see & interact.
  • How to consciously adopt & nurture a growth mindset.
  • How to move through conflict to connection.
  • How to balance your life priorities.
  • Why what you think/say/do matters.

Social Innovation & the Global Context

Deepen & expand your impact by:

  • Diving deeper into the issue & mission of your organization, & how it is part of & supports a larger movement.
  • Learning how to design, develop & execute effect campaigns.
  • Learning how to advocate for changes at the policy level.
  • Bolstering your sense of agency by gaining an understanding of our current systems & institutions & creating an action plan for how to individually & collectively create change.

Level up your leadership:

  • Organization-specific training to be effective leaders & volunteers in your respective organization.

The Methods

Cohorts

By grouping participants into cohorts that connect 1-2 times a day, we can design a more intimate experience that facilitates deeper relationships & encourages post-event engagement.

Keynotes

The theme, energy, & inspiration of Catalyst 2018 will be a held together by the shared experience of inspiring keynotes on each day.

Intensive Co-Led Skills Workshops

Our organizations deliver a lot of the same content at our annual training or retreat events. By capitalizing on shared needs/skills, we can learn from each other while & collaboratively participating in workshops.

Hands-On Training

We are committed to our shared cause by being committed to each of our individual causes. There will be time & space for breakout sessions so each organization’s specific training needs are met.

Exercises in Play, Co-Learning & Dialogue, & Visioning

Learning is something that you do, not something that happens to you. By facilitating a holistic & experiential learning process, the skills & lessons stick.

Community-Building Exercises & Space to Connect

There will be plenty of time to connect with fellow participants & organizational representatives so you can walk away from the event with a community you can’t wait to keep in touch with.
